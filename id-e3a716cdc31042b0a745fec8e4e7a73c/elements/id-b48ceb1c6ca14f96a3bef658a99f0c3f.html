Goal ()
A high-level statement of intent, direction, or desired state for an organization and its stakeholders. Goals are expressed qualitatively, and can represent anything a stakeholder may desire, such as a state of affairs, or a produced value. Goals define direction and intention, and may be broken into smaller sub-goals or linked to measurable outcomes. Goals are typically used to measure success of an organization and it is common to associate concrete outcomes with goals. Examples: * Universal access to personal health records * Optimize Supply Chain Management * Enhance Climate Resilience with Predictive Capabilities
